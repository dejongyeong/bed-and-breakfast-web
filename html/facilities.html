Facilities

Dining Room

Breakfast is served in our spacious dining room offering a choice of juice, cereal, fuit and yogurts followed by a choice of freshly cooked breakfast or continental if preferred. We also cater for vegetarians and any special dietary needs.

Single Bedrooms*

Single occupancy of comfortable rooms.

Double Bedrooms*

Double Bedrooms with 1 double bed, suitable for 2 guests sharing

Family Bedrooms*

Family Bedrooms suitable for 2 guest 2 children, with 1 double bed for 2 people, and 2 single beds.

Supplied in Every Room

  • All of our bedrooms have private en-suite bathrooms, with toilet and shower.
  • Tea & coffee making facilities in every room.
  • Towels and soap are supplied each day, as required.
  • Hairdryers, and an iron with ironing board is always available free of charge. Ask at the front desk as needs be.
  • All bedrooms are equipped with direct dial telephones, local radio & 12 channel satellite television.
  • All bedrooms & bathrooms are meticulously serviced, tidied & cleaned on a daily basis.

Other facilities

  • Wi-Fi Internet Access in all rooms.
  • Slippers on request. Ask at the front desk as needs be.
  • Allocated Smoking Area.
  • Private parking area for guests.
  • Trip planning advice around Waterville, Kerry
  • Cages for pets upon booking if needed.
  • Vouchers and Credit Cards accepted for payment
  • 24 hours reception.
  • CCTV Security.

*Rooms subject to availability
