Welcome to Juzzx Bed and Breakfast

Juzzx Bed and Breakfast is a three star Irish Home Bed and Brekfast located in Waterville, Kerry. A member of B&B Ireland and registered with the Irish Tourist Board, Failte Ireland. Being newly renovated Juzzx B&B is proud to boast of its new modern chic design, clealiness and economically savvy rates.

Situated in the picturesque coastal village of Waterville on The Wild Atlantic Way. It is a short walk to the Town center where you will find shops and restaurants

We will within reasonable means strive to provide a comfortable and memorable stay with us.
